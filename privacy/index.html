1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information

We collect various types of personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, display name, profile picture, and any other information you choose to provide in your profile.
  • Authentication Information: We collect information from third-party authentication providers (Google, Apple) when you sign in using these services, including your email address, name, and profile information.
  • Social Media Information: If you choose to link your social media accounts (Instagram, TikTok, X/Twitter), we may collect your social media profile information, including usernames, profile pictures, and public profile data.
  • Communication Information: We collect information from your communications with us, including support requests, feedback, and any other correspondence.

1.2 Content and Media

We collect and process various types of content and media that you create, upload, or share through our Service:

  • Photos and Images: We collect photos you upload for virtual try-on experiences, wardrobe management, outfit sharing, and profile pictures. This includes clothing items, outfit photos, and any other images you choose to share.
  • Virtual Try-On Data: We collect and process images used for virtual try-on generation, including uploaded clothing items, body photos, and AI-generated try-on results.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect content you create, including outfit combinations, style preferences, comments, likes, shares, and any other content you post or share through the Service.
  • Wardrobe Information: We collect information about your clothing items, including categories, brands, colors, sizes, and any metadata you provide about your wardrobe.

1.3 Usage and Analytics Data

We automatically collect certain information about how you use our Service:

  • 1.3.1 App Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the App, including features used, time spent in the app, pages visited, buttons clicked, and navigation patterns.
  • 1.3.2 Virtual Try-On Analytics: We collect data about your virtual try-on usage, including frequency of use, types of clothing items tried on, success rates, and user preferences.
  • 1.3.3 Performance Data: We collect technical data about app performance, including crash reports, error logs, loading times, and system performance metrics.
  • 1.3.4 Feature Usage: We track which features you use most frequently, including wardrobe management, social features, shopping integration, and style recommendations.
