Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

1. Introduction

We (the two creators of StyleWise) are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (“App”).

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile photo.
  • User Content: Photos you upload, wardrobe items, outfit data, and any other content you add to the App.
  • Usage Data: Device information, app usage statistics, and crash logs.
  • Affiliate Interactions: When you click on affiliate links in the shop section.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App’s core features.
  • To personalize your experience and save your wardrobe/outfit data.
  • To improve the App’s performance and user experience.
  • To communicate with you about your account or important updates.
  • To track affiliate link usage (for commission purposes).

4. How We Share Your Information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share your information with service providers (like Supabase for backend/database, AdMob for ads, and analytics providers) only as necessary for app functionality.
  • We may share your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Affiliate Links

  • Some links in the shop section are affiliate links. If you click these links and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no extra cost to you.
  • We only partner with brands that are affiliate/marketing friendly.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information at any time via the App. For other requests, contact us at stylewise@styleswise.io.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: stylewise@styleswise.io
