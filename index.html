How It Works

1
Step 1: Remove Backgrounds

Easily remove backgrounds from your clothing photos with our AI powered background remover. Upload your items and watch as our technology automatically isolates them, creating clean, professional images for your digital wardrobe.

2
Step 2: AI Outfit Suggestions

Our AI generates stylish outfit combinations based on the clothes already in your wardrobe. Discover new looks and get daily outfit inspiration tailored to your unique collection.

3
Step 3: Virtual Try-On

Experience your outfits before wearing them with our virtual try on feature. See how different clothing combinations look on you, mix and match pieces, and experiment with styles all from the comfort of your device.

4
Step 4: Curated Collection

Access your curated collection of clothing items that perfectly match your style preferences. Organize your wardrobe by season, occasion, and color palette for effortless daily dressing.

5
Step 5: Virtual Outfit Room

Step into your virtual outfit room where you can mix and match pieces to create stunning combinations. Visualize complete looks and get instant feedback on your style choices before making any purchases.

6
Step 6: Shop & Try On

Discover big shops and try on their clothes virtually before buying them. Shop with confidence and find the perfect fit and style for you all from the comfort of your device.
