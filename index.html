Frequently Asked Questions

How does the credit system work? ▼
Credits are used to access premium features like AI hairstyles, makeup, and virtual try-ons. Free users get 1 credit, while subscribers get monthly credits. You can also purchase credits or earn them through referrals.
What's the difference between subscription plans? ▼
Free: 1 credit, ads shown. Plus: 10 credits/month, ad-free. Pro: 30 credits/month, ad-free, priority support. Elite: Unlimited credits, all features, exclusive templates.
Can I use my own photos? ▼
Yes! Upload your photo and our AI will apply hairstyles or makeup to your image. All processing happens securely on our servers.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your photos or data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
What is SW Token? ▼
SW Token (StyleWise Token) is our native cryptocurrency built on Solana blockchain. It bridges AI-powered fashion with Web3 technology, turning creativity into real value. Earn tokens through app engagement, spend them on premium features, and be part of the future of digital fashion.
How to become a creator? ▼
To become a creator, you need to meet specific requirements including follower count and engagement metrics. Once approved, creators can create custom templates for users to try and earn based on template usage tiers. For more information, please check the app: Go to Profile → Settings → Become Creator section.
