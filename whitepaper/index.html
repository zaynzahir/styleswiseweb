Executive Summary

StyleWise is an AI-driven digital wardrobe and virtual try-on platform that turns everyday fashion interactions into measurable on-platform value and community rewards. The platform combines wardrobe management, AI outfit generation, AR/virtual try-on, and social features in a mobile app — and plans to layer a Solana-native token (SW) to drive engagement, reward creators, enable in-app purchases, and bootstrap a Web3 fashion economy.

Key Vision

The token is designed to be earned through app activity, withdrawable to user wallets, and tradable on Solana DEXs, creating a sustainable ecosystem where fashion creativity meets blockchain technology.

2

Problem & Market Opportunity

Problems for Consumers and Retailers

  • Online apparel purchase returns and dissatisfaction remain high, with industry sources showing apparel return rates well above other categories
  • Virtual try-on can reduce uncertainty and returns, addressing both consumer satisfaction and environmental impact
  • Current fashion discovery lacks personalization and community-driven recommendations

Market Opportunity

The global virtual try-on and AR shopping market is growing rapidly, with multiple market reports estimating multi-billion market size with strong CAGR. Entering now positions StyleWise to capture user, retail, and creator value as AR and AI adoption in retail accelerates.

Market Potential

Industry projections show significant growth potential through 2030, with virtual try-on technology becoming increasingly mainstream in e-commerce and social commerce platforms.

3

Product & Technology Overview

Core Product Components

  • Digital wardrobe & organizer: Users upload garments with background removal and build a personal closet
  • AI outfit suggestions: Personalized outfit generation using AI that factors user profile and local weather
  • Virtual try-on (AR): AR and virtual try-on to preview items on the user, plus a flexible canvas for mixing pieces
  • Community / social layer: Sharing looks, following creators, liking community content to drive discovery and engagement

Planned Web3 Integration

StyleWise Token (SW) is a Solana-native token with 1,000,000,000 total supply allocated across public markets, in-app rewards, marketing, and development. Users will earn SW via app engagement and withdraw to connected Solana wallets once minimum thresholds are reached.

Technical Decisions & Rationale

  • Blockchain: Solana chosen for low transaction costs and fast confirmation, practical for micro-rewards and frequent small interactions
  • AI/AR stack: Hybrid on-device + cloud inference for low-latency UI and heavier model runs, with careful privacy and performance management
4

Token Design & Utility

Token Overview

StyleWise Token (SW) is designed to create real utility within the fashion ecosystem while providing users with tangible value for their engagement and creativity.

Utility & Mechanics

  • Earn: Daily challenges, content creation, community engagement, and successful referrals
  • Spend (on-platform): Premium AI features, creator items, boosted visibility, and special AR filters
  • Withdraw / trade: Users can connect Solana wallets and withdraw earned tokens, tradable on Solana DEXs

Token Allocation

Total supply of 1,000,000,000 SW allocated as follows: 80% public/market, 10% in-app rewards & monetization, 5% marketing, 5% development.

Design Considerations

  • Minimum withdrawal thresholds and batched withdrawals to reduce on-chain costs and UX friction
  • Robust anti-abuse and bot detection to prevent gaming reward systems
  • Future on-chain governance for community proposals and creator fund oversight

Roadmap & Milestones

Phase 1 — Launch & Foundation

Token launch, website development, legal setup, and community channel establishment. Success metrics include token listing, audit scheduling, and community growth across social platforms.

Phase 2 — Community & Ecosystem Growth

Campaigns, creator onboarding, social content, and ambassador programs. Focus on DAU/MAU growth, retention rates, and creator engagement.

Phase 3 — In-App Integration

Wallet connection, earning flows, and withdrawal mechanism implementation. Metrics include percentage of active users earning tokens and withdrawal volume.

Phase 4 — Monetization & Utility Expansion

Premium features behind SW, staking mechanisms, and creator rewards marketplace. Focus on ARPU growth and conversion rates to paid features.

Phase 5 — Governance & Long-Term Vision

Community governance, creator fund, ecosystem grants, and enterprise partnerships. Metrics include governance proposals passed and third-party integrations.

6

Go-to-Market Strategy

Initial Channels (Consumer)

App Store and Play Store optimization with the app already live on iOS with positive ratings. Use social proof and creator partnerships to drive installs and organic virality.

Community & Token Mechanics

Reward early adopters with SW, airdrop creator grants, and incentivize referral growth loops. Implement token staking/boosts that reward long-term retention.

Retail & B2B Sales

Pilot integration with boutique retailers and third-party marketplaces to demonstrate conversion uplift and return reduction using ROI case studies for enterprise licensing.

Partnerships

Collaborate with AR SDK providers, 3D model marketplaces, fashion influencers, and Solana ecosystem projects including wallets and liquidity providers.

Transparency & Security

Compliance & Privacy

We comply with applicable regulations and prioritize user privacy. Our platform implements strict privacy policies, on-device processing where possible, and transparent data retention policies.

Platform Integrity

We actively monitor for fair play and platform integrity. Our systems include anti-fraud measures, rate limiting, and identity verification for high-value transactions.

Technical Security

Our technical infrastructure prioritizes security with regular audits, secure wallet integration, and robust data protection measures to ensure user safety and platform reliability.

Our Commitment

StyleWise is committed to building a transparent, secure, and user-centric platform that empowers creators while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.
